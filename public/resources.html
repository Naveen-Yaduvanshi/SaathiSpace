Mental Health Resources

Helpful information and simple guidance for different challenges.

Depression

Depression can cause low mood, lack of energy, and loss of interest. Small steps like sunlight, movement, and talking to someone can help.

  • Go outside for 10–15 minutes daily
  • Maintain a simple routine
  • Write your feelings in a journal

Anxiety

Anxiety causes racing thoughts, tension, and worry. Breathing and grounding exercises can calm the body.

  • Practice 4-4-4 breathing
  • Use the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique
  • Limit caffeine

Loneliness

Loneliness happens when we feel emotionally disconnected. Small social actions can slowly reduce this feeling.

  • Talk to one person daily
  • Join online communities
  • Spend time in public spaces

Panic Attacks

Panic attacks feel intense but usually pass within minutes. Slow breathing helps the nervous system calm down.

  • Focus on slow breathing
  • Remind yourself: “This will pass”
  • Sit in a safe, quiet place

Professional Help

If your feelings are overwhelming or long-lasting, consider talking to a mental health professional.

  • Psychologists
  • Psychiatrists
  • Counselors
